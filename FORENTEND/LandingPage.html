About TodoList

Our Mission

At TodoList, we believe that productivity shouldn't be complicated. Our mission is to provide a simple, elegant, and powerful task management solution that helps you organize your life without getting in your way.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, TodoList was born from the frustration of using overcomplicated productivity apps. We wanted to create something that felt natural, looked beautiful, and actually helped people get things done.
